Happy 100,000 Times :)

A visualization & exploration of happy moments

By Daisy Chen

What’s the data of happiness?

You might have heard of World Happiness Report in which Finland is ranked No.1 with the highest score. The score is based on several different aspects including social support, healthy life expectancy etc. And there is also research on the correlation between happiness and life satisfaction, happiness overtime...

What would you think of when talking about happiness?

Friends, family, partner, pets, … These are concrete details that leads you directly be memory of happiness. Here, instead of showing you the abstract numbers, this data visualization brings you to the moment of happiness and allows you to explore among different happy moments.

Please enjoy :)
