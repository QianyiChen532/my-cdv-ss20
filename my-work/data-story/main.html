How to explore

Chart: You'll see 2000 happy moments among the 100,000 happy moments. X axis is the age of the individual who wrote down the happy moment. Y axis are categories of that happy moment.

Reset: Everytime you click on the reset button bellow you'll get different random happy moments.

Hover: put the mouse on the circle and see the detail of happy moment

Brush: select a certain age range to view using the grey rectangle.

Filter: use the checkbox below to check some specific topics/words!(*in some cases a subject may not have matched points)
