What made you happy over the past 24h/3months?

Over 10,000 people documented their 100,000 happy moments and made this wonderful dataset called happyDB. Each datapoint contains is basically a message about happy moment, a predicted category, and some basic demographic information related to the person(just for academic analysis use).

Therefore, my design for the visualization of this data is also mainly based on the category, the individuals, and the message(text) itself.

Category

The categories of the happy moments are predicted by some NLP tools based on several rounds of calculations and classifications. The estimations are rough, but in my scans, they appear to be a decent baseline and are interesting enough to look at more closely.

Category count for different predicted category

Affection and achievement are listed as the top2, which is close to our own experience.

Subject

Apparently, most of the happy moments are about “I” or “we”, but in addition to that, other subjects involved in this moment are also worth looking at.

I especially like the "someone" moment.

Subject count for some keywords

Text

The researchers built this corpus with the goal to understand the causes of happiness and how can techniques like NLP contribute to positive psychology. But since there is enough pattern analysis, in this experience I also want the audience to fully immerse in the text and feel the power of the text itself.
